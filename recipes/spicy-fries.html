Spicy Cajun Fries

Description

French fries are a beloved classic all around the world, espically in the United States. Why not spice it up with some Cajun flavors?

Ingredients

  • 4 large russet potatoes, peeled and cut into 1/4 inch thick fries
  • 1/4 cup veggie oil
  • 1/4 cup tomato-veggie juice
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 2 teaspoons dried onion granules
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon salt

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 F (190 C). Grease a large baking sheet
  2. Fill large bowl with cold water, add potatoes, allow to soak for 10 minutes.
  3. Whisk together oil, vegetable juice, chili powder, ground cumin, onion, garlic, cayenne, sugar, and salt, in a large bowl.
  4. Drain potatoes and pat dry, toss in the oil and spice mixture until evenly coated. Put on baking sheet in single layer.
  5. Cook for 20 minutes, flip and bake for another 20, until brown and crispy.
