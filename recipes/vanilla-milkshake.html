Vanilla Milkshake

Description

An all American classic, completely indulgent and decandent. If you want to go the extra level, why not dip some fries in it?..

Ingredients

  • 2 cups vanilla ice cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Blend Ingredients in blender until smooth, pour into glass and serve
