Tomato Sandwich

Description

Crunchy warm toast, fresh juicy tomatoes, and a tangy creamy sauce with some pep. What is not to love?

Ingredients

  1. 2 tablespoons ketchup
  2. 1 tablespoon mayonnaise
  3. 1/8th teaspoon salt
  4. 1/8th teaspoon hot pepper sauce
  5. 4 slices whole wheat bread
  6. 2 slices lettuce
  7. 1 tomato, sliced

Directions

  1. Mix together ketchup, mayo, salt and hot sauce in a bowl
  2. Toast bread, spread the sauce on all four slices
  3. Place lettuce on piece of toast, top with 3-4 slices of tomato, cover with another toast slice. Repeat for the other sandwich.
